Bouquet & Basket Flower Creations Activities

Come and join our Bouquet & Basket Flower Creations Activities, where you can learn to make your own stunning bouquet and basket of fresh flowers. Allow us to assist you in bringing happiness to someone's day with beautiful floral designs.

Floral Design & Garden Classes

Join our garden-style floral design and gardening classes to learn the best techniques and mechanics. Our classes will equip you with the skills needed to create stunning floral arrangements at various price points. You'll also learn about design principles and elements that will enable you to express your creativity in your floral designs. Additionally, we offer gardening classes and advice to assist parents in establishing and maintaining a flourishing and environmentally friendly garden. As a beginner, we'll even supply you with a complimentary garden toolset kit to help you get started.
